Description:

This is the USS Enterprise as depicted in Star Trek: Remastered. Though this ship does not feature animated bussards, the model is very accurate and has crisp textures.

Installation Instructions:

1. After installing BC v1.1, and the foundation mod, unzip this mod into a temporary directory.

2. Copy files (data, scripts, and sfx) into your Bridge Commander folder. Click "Yes" to overwrite.

3. Once ingame, you should see a new ship selection called WC TOS Ships and within it you will find WC TOS Constitution, then WC TOS Enterprise. Select the ship and enjoy!
