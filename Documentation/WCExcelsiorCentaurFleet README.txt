Description:

This is the entire fleet of the Excelsior-Class starships and the Centaur-Class ships seen in DS9. This pack features 30 ships spanning 3 eras- TMP, TNG, and DS9/Voyager. Every ship mentioned and featured in the Star Trek series is featured, and a few ship are added for fun also. Most of the Excelsior ships feature the warp coil glow first seen in the Star Trek: Voyager episode "Flashback." Please check the readme for requirements to fully enjoy this large pack of ships.

Installation Instructions:

1. After installing BC v1.1, and the foundation mod, unzip this mod into a temporary directory.

2. Copy files (data, scripts, and sfx) into your Bridge Commander folder. Click "Yes" to overwrite.

3. Once ingame, you should see new ship selections called Excelsior Class and Centaur Class (or something like that.....you'll know). Select your ship and enjoy!
