Bridge Commander Construction Ship

This is A brand new version of the federation Construction ship. Like it's armada 2 counterpart,
the ship has light shields and armaments however, it is also fitted with a Phaser and Warp Drive, 
The ship can also use MVAM to open it's constructionbays which are large enough for two or three 
small shuttle craft to fly through.
