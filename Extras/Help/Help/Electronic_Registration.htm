Electronic Registration

Note: Electronic Registration and any accompanying offers are for use in North America ONLY.

There are three ways to access electronic registration.

  1. Select Register Now during the installation process.

  2. Register from the Star Trek®: Bridge Commander™ Launch screen:

    1. Insert the CD.

    2. When the title screen appears, click Help/Support.

    3. Click Register.

  3. Register from the Start Menu:

        a.  Click on the Windows Start button.

        b.  Select Programs.

        c.  Select Star Trek Bridge Commander.

        d.  Click on Register.
