Contact your video card manufacturer for details.

NOTES:  

Star Trek: Bridge Commander does not limit the resolutions supported under Windows®.  If Windows is configured with the wrong monitor settings,  then you may experience problems in Star Trek: Bridge Commander, when trying to select the higher resolutions.  This will result in a black screen when you select these resolutions in Star Trek: Bridge Commander.  If you cannot display in high resolution in Windows then you cannot display in high resolution inside Star Trek: Bridge Commander.  This is a limitation of the monitor and not Star Trek: Bridge Commander.

Star Trek: Bridge Commander does not limit the resolutions supported on secondary graphics cards (i.e. Voodoo 2).  If your monitor can only display in 640x480 resolution, then it is suggested that you don't select higher resolutions when setting the mode for your secondary card.
